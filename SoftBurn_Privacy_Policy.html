Privacy Policy for SoftBurn

The SoftBurn app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Data Collection

  • No Personal Data Collected: SoftBurn does not collect any personal information, such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • No Tracking or Analytics: SoftBurn does not gather or track any data, including anonymized usage statistics or analytics.
  • No Access to Sensitive Device Features: SoftBurn does not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

Third-Party Services

  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: Since SoftBurn does not collect any personal data or display any advertising, it does not share any data with third-party services.

Permissions

  • Photos Library Access: SoftBurn may request read-only access to your Photos Library in order to display your photos and videos in slideshows. This access is entirely optional, and SoftBurn never modifies your original media.
  • File System Access: SoftBurn can open photos and videos from folders you select. Access is limited to files and folders you explicitly choose.

Contact
